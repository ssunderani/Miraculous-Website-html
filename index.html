ABOUT THE BOOK

A BATTLE THAT CHANGED EVERYTHING

On May 25, 2011, sixty U.S. and Afghan Soldiers were dropped by helicopter into the remote, enemy-held Do Ab District Center of Nuristan, Afghanistan. Their mission: a simple reconnaissance. But within minutes of landing, they were ambushed.

Pinned down, outnumbered more than five to one, and taking fire from all directions, the platoon was trapped in a kill zone with nowhere to run. For the next seven hours, they would have to rely on their training, courage, and each other to withstand an overwhelming onslaught from over 300 Taliban fighters.

This is a story of incredible bravery, impossible odds, and the unwavering bond between Soldiers who faced death and refused to break.

ABOUT THE AUTHORS

TWO VOICES, ONE STORY

A father who wrote to understand the war his son fought. A commander who lived the ground truth of it.

AUTHOR · JOURNALIST

JOHN KELLING

Graduate of Minnesota State University, Mankato, with a background in broadcasting and journalism. He worked with WHO Radio, ESPN, Fox Sports, and the Big Ten Network. His writing about his son's Afghanistan deployment led to his first book, Hope is a Weapon.

U.S. ARMY · COMBAT VETERAN

LTC JUSTIN FOOTE

Justin Foote has served more than 28 years in the United States Army, rising from enlisted ranks to earn his commission. A combat veteran of Afghanistan, he writes from firsthand experience about leadership, service, family, sacrifice, and brotherhood.

More about John Kelling

John Kelling has spent decades capturing human interest stories across various broadcasting mediums. In "Miraculous Turn of Events", he brings a father's perspective to combat literature, capturing the emotional and psychological journey of a family whose son is deployed to a combat zone.

More about LTC Justin Foote

Throughout his distinguished military career, LTC Foote received numerous awards for valor and service. He resides in the United States and continues to support veteran organizations and speak on leadership and military history.
